About

BIG RED BIKES


Our Mission...

Big Red Bikes's mission is to advocate for and implement sustainable transportation that is accessible, efficient, and affordable for all members of the Cornell community on campus. We coordinate with Cornell Transportation Services on proposing and managing a bike share program. We also host a variety of workshops and events to teach the public on bike safety and maintenance and promote sustainable transportation services. Students will have the opportunity to gain experience in bike sharing operations, planning, grant writing, marketing, and community engagement.

What Can You Do?

A Lot!

  • Ride a bike to class/work instead of driving a car!
  • Encourage others to bike, it is fun after all and we have a beautiful campus!
  • Engage in the community and make what you want known; if you want more biking options on campus, make it known!

Want to get involved?

Contact us if you would like to join us, we are always looking for new members who are passionate about making a real impact on the campus and to promote various bike programs on campus!
